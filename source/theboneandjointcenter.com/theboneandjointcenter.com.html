Welcome to The Bone & Joint Center

Share

Due to inclement weather, we have temporarily lost power at our Albany location, and therefore, we have closed our office and Orthopaedic Urgent Care for the remainder of the day. We expect to resume normal business hours tomorrow, Saturday, July 28.

Since 1963, the doctors and surgeons of The Bone & Joint Center have offered orthopaedic excellence and exceptional care to every patient.

Our specialty-trained doctors of The Bone & Joint Center provide treatment for orthopaedic injuries and conditions affecting the ankle, back, elbow, foot, hand, hip, knee, neck, shoulder, spine, and wrist in addition to specializing in general orthopaedics, orthopaedic oncology, physical medicine and rehabilitation, sports medicine, total joint replacement and revision, and trauma.

Our world-class team of orthopaedic experts is devoted to providing customized treatment plans focused on your needs and goals.

Thank you for giving us the opportunity to serve you. We do not take it for granted, and we hope you’ll extend us the privilege again in the future.

At The Bone & Joint Center, we make you stronger.
